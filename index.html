Cristiano Ronaldo at the 2022 FIFA World Cup.

Here is a time-line of major events Cristiano Ronaldo's life and career:

  • 6 February 1985: Born in Madeira, Portugal
  • 2 July 1993: Plays for his first amateur team
  • 8 October 2002: Makes his professional debut.
  • 12 August 2003: Transfer to Manchester United
  • 16 August 2003: Premier League debut
  • 20 August 2003: International Debut
  • 9 September 2003: UEFA Champions League debut
  • 1 November 2003: First Premier League goal
  • 26 September 2006: First UEFA Champions League goal
  • 22 May 2004: First trophy (FA Cup)
  • 11 June 2006: First World Cup appearance
  • 17 June 2006: First World Cup goal
  • 12 January 2008: First hat-trick (Against Newcastle United)
  • 2 December 2008: First Ballon d'Or win
  • 11 June 2009: Transfer to Real Madrid
  • 29 August 2009: Real Madrid debut
  • 29 August 2009: First goal for Real Madrid
  • 17 October 2015: All-time Real Madrid top-scorer
  • 10 July 2016: Euro Cup win
  • 7 December 2017: Fifth Ballon d'Or
  • 26 May 2018: Fifth Champions League
  • 15 June 2018: First World Cup hat-trick
  • 18 August 2018: Serie A debut for Juventus
  • 16 September 2018: Serie A first goal
  • 12 May 2021: 100th Juventus goal
  • 23 May 2021: Serie A Golden Boot
  • 27 August 2021: Return to Manchester United
  • 1 September 2021: All-time international top-scorer
  • 2 December 2021: 800th Career goal
  • 16 April 2022: 50th club hat-trick
  • 23 April 2022: 100th Premier League goal
